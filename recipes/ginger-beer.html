Ginger Beer

Simple and Spicy to help your throat and Tummy

I had started making this with the intentions on making it alcoholic, which I have been able to ferment once or twice. It usually gets drank by the Wife and Neice before it gets that Chance. This can also be blended up with some frozen Strawberries to make the Famous 'Ginger Berry Cooler'

STUFF

  • 2-3 lbs(4-6 Kg) of Ginger
  • 15 Green Limes
  • Citrus Press
  • Ninja Blender
  • pear Juice
  • clean water
  • big contianer
  • Brown Sugar
  • Strainer, funel, micro-mesh filter

STEPS

  1. Clean Ginger, break apart into fingers and put half ammount in Ninja
  2. Add Cup of Brown Sugar
  3. Slice limes in half and juice about 8 of them into Ninja
  4. Fill Ninja to Max Liquid level with clean water
  5. Blend throughly until just pulp and liquid
  6. Strain and funel into jug, squeeze out as much as can with glass againse strainer
  7. once all Liquid from ninja has been strained, add spent ginger back into Ninja
  8. Add remaining Ginger, juice remaining limes, and another cup of sugar and fill with water
  9. Blend again and strain into Jug
  10. SPent Ginger should be saved and Dehydrated to make Ginger Powder
  11. Full bottle of Pear Juice should be just enough to top off the jug
  12. Shake and frigerate, usually lasts about a week unless fermented properly
