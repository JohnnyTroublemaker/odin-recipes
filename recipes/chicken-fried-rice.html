Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken and Rice is Mighty nice

I started doing this recipe after watching some Uncle Roger vijeos on YouTube I paid close attention and I think I nailed it, if not made it better. Even if I am being biast

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Jasmine Rice
  • Seasame Oil
  • 4 Cups of Water
  • Baking soda
  • white vinegar
  • Ginger Powder
  • Bacon Fat
  • Soy Sauce
  • 5 cloves of garlic - Minced
  • 1/2 Shallot - Diced
  • Curry Powder
  • Oyster sauce
  • fish sauce
  • MSG
  • 3 Eggs - Scrambled
  • White Pepper
  • 2-3 Butterflied Chicken Breast
  • 4-5 Sliced Button Mushrooms
  • Green onion chives

Steps

  1. Cook Rice, as it takes the longest, It can be cooked ahead of time, but I find little difference the way I cook the rice
  2. Throughly Rince the rice through a strainer and Tap water until runs clear
  3. Bring 4 cups of Water in Cast Iron pan, and bring to boil
  4. Season water with Ginger and Curry powders
  5. once boiling, reduce heat to low and stir in rinced rice, then cover for 18 minutes. Once done, remove lid, fluff, recover and place aside.
  6. While Rice is cooking, we will velvet the Chicken, place butterflied chicken in zip lock back with a few tablespoons of Baking powder and then slowly add White vinegar. This will violently Bubble
  7. Once gas exchange calms down add enough vinegar to cover chicken, squeeze out air, 90% seal the bag and place to the side, upright
  8. Chop Shallots, Mushrooms, Garlic
  9. Heat Cast Iron skillet until water beads like mercury
  10. add seasame seed oil, bacon fat and aromatics
  11. Rinse chicken and grill in Skillet, season with spices
  12. once cooked, add oil and Scrambled egg in corner of skillet
  13. stir in Egg with meat and aromatics, and ladel in Rice
  14. Add Soy until color is just right and saltiness desired
  15. Add chopped Green onions for garnish and crunch
